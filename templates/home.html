Watchdog

Stay independent and live without fear



Forget medical devices.

The first thing you do every morning shouldn't be putting a reminder of your mortality around your neck. Watchdog is a app for the Pebble smartwatch, a modern and featureful version of a familiar, time-honored accessory. Keep up with the Joneses and outlive them, too.

Growing old happens to everyone,

but that doesn't mean you have to lose your independence. Watchdog lets you live without being afraid that a bad fall could be the last one you have. When Watchdog detects potential trauma, your watch will begin "barking" for an amount of time you specify. If you can't turn it off, your trusted contacts will be notified that you need help and also where you need it. If you're suddenly incapicitated by a stroke or fall, you can be sure that you'll get the timely help you need.

As loyal as any dog in the world

Watchdog's vigilance combined with the ruggedness of Pebble lets you be sure that you won't find yourself fighting for life alone. As long as Watchdog is with you, your loved ones will never be too far away.
